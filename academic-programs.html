ACADEMIC PROGRAMS

TAW College for Women offers a range of programs designed to empower women with necessary skills and knowledge

B.Com (General)

The Bachelor of Commerce (General) is a comprehensive three-year undergraduate program that offers foundational knowledge in commerce, business, finance, and economics. This program is designed to cultivate analytical thinking, business acumen, and professional skills to prepare students for a dynamic global marketplace.

Through a balanced curriculum that integrates theoretical frameworks with practical applications, students gain a holistic understanding of core business disciplines. The program also emphasizes critical thinking, communication, problem-solving, and ethical decision-making.

Course Details

Duration: 3 Years (6 Semesters)

Mode: Full-Time

Eligibility: Candidates who have completed 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board, with Commerce or any other stream.

Key Highlights
  • Curriculum aligned with industry standards and emerging business trends
  • Courses in Accounting, Economics, Business Law, Marketing, and Taxation
  • Focus on practical learning through case studies, presentations, and projects
  • Opportunities for internships, workshops, and certification programs
  • Expert faculty with academic and industry experience
  • Skill development in communication, finance, and data analysis
Why Choose Our B.Com (General) Program?
  • Industry-relevant curriculum that bridges academic knowledge with real-world application
  • Dynamic learning environment fostering innovation and leadership
  • Regular industry interactions and seminars to enhance practical exposure
  • Holistic development through co-curricular and extra-curricular activities
  • Career guidance and placement support to empower student success
Career Scope & Opportunities

The B.Com General program offers vast career opportunities across multiple sectors. Graduates are equipped to take on roles in corporate, financial, governmental, and entrepreneurial settings.


Popular Career Paths:


  • Accountant
  • Financial Analyst
  • Tax Consultant
  • Business Development Executive
  • Banking and Insurance Professional
  • Investment Advisor
  • Entrepreneur
  • Office Administrator

Graduates are also well-positioned to pursue higher education and professional qualifications, including:


  • Master of Commerce (M.Com)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Chartered Accountancy (CA)
  • Company Secretary (CS)
  • Cost and Management Accountancy (CMA)
  • Certified Financial Planner (CFP)
  • Civil and Banking Service Examinations (UPSC, SSC, IBPS, etc.)
Start your journey toward a successful career in commerce and business with our B.Com General program.

B.Sc Computer Science

Course Details

Duration: 3 Years (6 Semesters)

Mode: Full-Time

Eligibility: Candidates must have completed 10+2 or equivalent from a recognised board with Mathematics/Computer Science.

Key Highlights

  • Training in programming languages like C, C++, Python, and Java
  • Lab sessions, coding marathons, and technical workshops
  • Industry-oriented certifications and add-on courses
  • Guest lectures, webinars, and mentorship by IT professionals
  • Internship & project work to build a strong portfolio
What You’ll Explore
  • Programming fundamentals and problem-solving techniques
  • Data structures, algorithms, and software development
  • Database systems and computer networks
  • Web development and cloud computing basics
  • Cybersecurity principles and ethical practices
  • Artificial Intelligence and emerging technologies
Career Scope & Opportunities

Graduates of this program are equipped for diverse roles in the tech industry, such as:


  • Software Developer
  • Web Developer
  • System Analyst
  • Database Administrator
  • Cloud Solutions Architect
  • IT Consultant or Entrepreneur

Technology shapes the future—become a creator, innovator, and leader in the digital world.

Join the B.Sc. Computer Science program and start your journey toward a rewarding tech career.

B.Sc. Psychology

The Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) in Psychology is a three-year undergraduate program designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of human behavior, mental processes, and emotional functioning. This program integrates scientific theories with practical applications to develop critical insights into psychological concepts and real-world challenges.

Through a balanced and interdisciplinary curriculum, students explore the biological, cognitive, social, and developmental aspects of psychology. The program emphasizes analytical thinking, research skills, communication, and ethical practices in the study and application of psychology.

Course Details

Duration: 3 Years (6 Semesters)

Mode: Full-Time

Eligibility: Candidates must have completed 10+2 or equivalent from a recognised board preferably with Psychology, Science, or any stream,.

Key Highlights
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering Cognitive Psychology, Abnormal Psychology, Developmental Psychology, Social Psychology, and Biological Bases of Behavior
  • Emphasis on practical training through laboratory work, internships, and research projects
  • Opportunities for field visits, case study analyses, and psychological assessments
  • Exposure to emerging areas such as Positive Psychology, Health Psychology, and Organizational Behavior
  • Experienced faculty with academic, clinical, and research backgrounds
  • Skill development in research methods, counseling techniques, critical analysis, and interpersonal communication
Why Choose Our B.Sc Psychology Program?
  • Rigorous and research-driven curriculum based on current psychological theories and practices
  • Interactive learning environment promoting critical thinking and empathy
  • Hands-on exposure through internships, workshops, and community outreach programs
  • Regular seminars, conferences, and expert talks to keep pace with contemporary psychological trends
  • Career mentoring, research guidance, and placement assistance for professional growth
Career Scope & Opportunities

The B.Sc Psychology program opens doors to diverse career pathways across healthcare, education, corporate, and research sectors. Graduates are equipped with the knowledge and skills to work in various professional settings or pursue higher studies.


Popular Career Paths:


  • Clinical Psychologist (with further qualification)
  • School Counselor
  • Human Resources Executive
  • Rehabilitation Specialist
  • Research Assistant
  • Behavioral Therapist
  • Mental Health Support Worker
  • Career Counselor

Graduates can also pursue advanced education and professional certifications, including:


  • Master of Science (M.Sc) in Psychology or specialized fields like Clinical, Counseling, or Organizational Psychology
  • Postgraduate Diplomas in Guidance and Counseling, Special Education, or Applied Psychology
  • Doctoral studies (Ph.D.) in Psychology
  • Professional training for roles in Mental Health, Psychotherapy, and Neuropsychology
  • Civil Service Examinations (UPSC, SSC, State Services)



Embark on a journey to understand the human mind and empower others.

Enroll in our B.Sc. Psychology program today!
