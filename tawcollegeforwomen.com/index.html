Honouring the Vision of Janab Alhaj T. Abdul Wahid Saheb

TAW College for Women is founded on the enduring legacy of Janab Alhaj T. Abdul Wahid Saheb and is dedicated to empowering women through education.

A visionary entrepreneur and philanthropist, Mr. T. Abdul Wahid significantly uplifted the community of Ambur Town with his remarkable contributions to education, politics, and business.

VISION and MISSION

VISION

Our vision is to empower every woman through transformative education that nurtures intellect, fosters independence, and prepares her to make a positive impact on her family and society. We are dedicated to cultivating leaders who drive meaningful change locally and globally

MISSION

Our mission is to empower women through accessible, affordable, and high-quality education that nurtures growth, builds skills, and fosters confidence. We aim to uplift individuals, families, and communities, creating lasting positive change.
